The REAP Project
Jolanta Szymakowski

The REAP Project (Realising the Potential of Australia's High Capacity Students) was a project undertaken by the Melbourne Graduate School of Education, The University of Melbourne 2015 - 2023.

This research was supported under the Australian Research Council's Linkage Projects funding scheme (LP140100236, Realising the Potential of Australia's High Capacity Students), in partnership with the State of Victoria as represented by the Department of Education and Training.
